My learning plan for this semester!

What was difficult last semester?

Deadlines and Time Management

Last semester I really struggled to meet course assignment deadlines. I couldn't manage my time properly due to my bad habits like sleeping late and waking up at 9am in the mornings. I often started assignments late, which made me feel rushed and stressed, and as a result, I couldn't have time to review my assignments and my works quality.

My action plan for learning

Building better study and work routines, and fixing my bad habits

I've made it a habit to begin each day with a plan. I stay ahead on my homework, and beyond my BCIT courses, I focus on personal growth by watching industry-related content on YouTube. Above all, I'm learning to enjoy my experience at BCIT and appreciate the journey.
